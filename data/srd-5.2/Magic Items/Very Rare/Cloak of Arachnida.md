# Cloak of Arachnida

_Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)_

This fine garment is made of black silk interwoven with faint, silvery threads. While wearing it, you gain the following benefits.

_**Poison Resistance.**_ You have Resistance to Poison damage.

_**Spider Climb.**_ You have a Climb Speed equal to your Speed and can move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and along ceilings, while leaving your hands free.

_**Spider Walk.**_ You can't be caught in webs of any sort and can move through webs as if they were Difficult Terrain.

_**Web.**_ You can cast _Web_ (save DC 13). The web created by the spell fills twice its normal area. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
