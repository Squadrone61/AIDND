# Periapt of Wound Closure

_Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)_

While wearing this pendant, you gain the following benefits.

_**Life Preservation.**_ Whenever you make a Death Saving Throw, you can change a roll of 9 or lower to a 10, turning a failed save into a successful one.

_**Natural Healing Boost.**_ Whenever you roll a Hit Point Die to regain Hit Points, double the number of Hit Points it restores.
