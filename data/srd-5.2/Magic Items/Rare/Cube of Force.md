# Cube of Force

_Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)_

This cube is about an inch across. Each face has a distinct marking on it. You can press one of those faces, expend the number of charges required for it, and thereby cast the spell associated with it (save DC 17), as shown in the table.

The cube starts with 10 charges, and it regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn.

| Spell              |   Charge Cost |
|--------------------|---------------|
| _Mage Armor_       |             1 |
| _Shield_           |             1 |
| _Tiny Hut_         |             3 |
| _Private Sanctum_  |             4 |
| _Resilient Sphere_ |             4 |
| _Wall of Force_    |             5 |
