# Pirate

_Medium or Small Humanoid, Neutral_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** +5 (15)
- **HP** 33 (6d8 + 6)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     10 |     16 |     12 |      8 |     12 |     14 |
| **Modifier**     |     +0 |     +3 |     +1 |     -1 |     +1 |     +2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +0 |     +5 |     +1 |     -1 |     +1 |     +4 |

- **Gear** Daggers (6), Leather Armor
- **Senses** Passive Perception 11
- **Languages** Common plus one other language
- **CR** 1 (XP 200; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The pirate makes two Dagger attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Enthralling Panache.

_**Dagger.**_ _Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft. _Hit:_ 5 (1d4 + 3) Piercing damage.

_**Enthralling Panache.**_ _Wisdom Saving Throw:_ DC 12, one creature the pirate can see within 30 feet. _Failure:_ The target has the Charmed condition until the start of the pirate's next turn.
