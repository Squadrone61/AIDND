- **Initiative** +3 (13)
- **HP** 22 (4d8 + 4)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|

- **Languages** Blink Dog; understands Elvish and Sylvan but can't speak them
- **CR** 1/4 (XP 50; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 5 (1d4 + 3) Piercing damage.

## Bonus Actions

_**Teleport (Recharge 4-6).**_ The dog teleports up to 40 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
