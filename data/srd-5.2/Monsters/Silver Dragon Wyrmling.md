# Silver Dragon Wyrmling

_Medium Dragon (Metallic), Lawful Good_

- **AC** 17
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 45 (6d8 + 18)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Fly 60 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     10 |     17 |     12 |     11 |     15 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +0 |     +3 |     +1 |     +0 |     +2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +2 |     +3 |     +1 |     +2 |     +2 |

- **Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +2
- **Immunities** Cold
- **Senses** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 14
- **Languages** Draconic
- **CR** 2 (450 XP; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The dragon makes two Rend attacks.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 9 (1d10 + 4) Piercing damage.

_**Cold Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Constitution Saving Throw:_ DC 13, each creature in a 15-foot Cone. _Failure:_ 18 (4d8) Cold damage. _Success:_ Half damage.

_**Paralyzing Breath.**_ _Constitution Saving Throw:_ DC 13, each creature in a 15-foot Cone. _First Failure:_ The target has the Incapacitated condition until the end of its next turn, when it repeats the save. _Second Failure:_ The target has the Paralyzed condition, and it repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically.
