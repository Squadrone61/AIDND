# Flying Snake

_Tiny Monstrosity, Unaligned_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 5 (2d4)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Fly 60 ft., Swim 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      4 |     15 |     11 |      2 |     12 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     -3 |     +2 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -3 |     +2 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |

- **Senses** Blindsight 10 ft.; Passive Perception 11
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1/8 (XP 25; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Flyby.**_ The snake doesn't provoke an Opportunity Attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +4, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 1 Piercing damage plus 5 (2d4) Poison damage.
