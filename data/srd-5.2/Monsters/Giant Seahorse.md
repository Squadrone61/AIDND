# Giant Seahorse

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** +1 (11)
- **HP** 16 (3d10)
- **Speed** 5 ft., Swim 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     15 |     12 |     11 |      2 |     12 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +1 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +1 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |

- **Senses** Passive Perception 11
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1/2 (XP 100; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Water Breathing.**_ The seahorse can breathe only underwater.

## Actions

_**Ram.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +4, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 9 (2d6 + 2) Bludgeoning damage, or 11 (2d8 + 2) Bludgeoning damage if the seahorse moved 20+ feet straight toward the target immediately before the hit.

## Bonus Actions

_**Bubble Dash.**_ While underwater, the seahorse moves up to half its Swim Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
