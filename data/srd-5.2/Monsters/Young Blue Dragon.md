# Young Blue Dragon

_Large Dragon (Chromatic), Lawful Evil_

- **AC** 18
- **Initiative** +4 (14)
- **HP** 152 (16d10 + 64)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Burrow 20 ft., Fly 80 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     21 |     10 |     19 |     14 |     13 |     17 |
| **Modifier**     |     +5 |     +0 |     +4 |     +2 |     +1 |     +3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +5 |     +4 |     +4 |     +2 |     +5 |     +3 |

- **Skills** Perception +9, Stealth +4
- **Immunities** Lightning
- **Senses** Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 120 ft.; Passive Perception 19
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **CR** 9 (XP 5,000; PB +4)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The dragon makes three Rend attacks.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +9, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 12 (2d6 + 5) Slashing damage plus 5 (1d10) Lightning damage.

_**Lightning Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Dexterity Saving Throw:_ DC 16, each creature in a 60-foot-long, 5-footwide Line. _Failure:_ 55 (10d10) Lightning damage. _Success:_ Half damage.
