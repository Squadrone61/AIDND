- **Initiative** +4 (14)
- **HP** 178 (17d10 + 85)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Climb 40 ft., Fly 80 ft.

| **Saving Throw** |     +6 |     +4 |     +5 |     +2 |     +4 |     +4 |

- **Languages** Common, Draconic

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The dragon makes three Rend attacks.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +10, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 13 (2d6 + 6) Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Fire damage.

_**Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Dexterity Saving Throw:_ DC 17, each creature in a 30-foot Cone. _Failure:_ 56 (16d6) Fire damage. _Success:_ Half damage.
