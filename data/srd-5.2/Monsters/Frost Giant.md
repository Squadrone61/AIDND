# Frost Giant

_Huge Giant, Neutral Evil_

- **AC** 15
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 149 (13d12 + 65)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     23 |      9 |     21 |      9 |     10 |     12 |
| **Modifier**     |     +6 |     -1 |     +5 |     -1 |     +0 |     +1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +6 |     -1 |     +8 |     -1 |     +3 |     +4 |

- **Skills** Athletics +9, Perception +3
- **Immunities** Cold
- **Senses** Passive Perception 13
- **Languages** Giant
- **CR** 8 (XP 3,900; PB +3)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The giant makes two attacks, using Frost Axe or Great Bow in any combination.

_**Frost Axe.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +9, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 19 (2d12 + 6) Slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) Cold damage.

_**Great Bow.**_ _Ranged Attack Roll:_ +9, range 150/600 ft. _Hit:_ 17 (2d10 + 6) Piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) Cold damage, and the target's Speed decreases by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

## Bonus Actions

_**War Cry (Recharge 5-6).**_ The giant or one creature of its choice that can see or hear it gains 16 (2d10 + 5) Temporary Hit Points and has Advantage on attack rolls until the start of the giant's next turn.
