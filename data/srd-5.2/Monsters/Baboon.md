## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The baboon has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the baboon's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +1, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 1 (1d4 - 1) Piercing damage.
