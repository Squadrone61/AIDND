# Air Elemental

_Large Elemental, Neutral_

- **AC** 15
- **Initiative** +5 (15)
- **HP** 90 (12d10 + 24)
- **Speed** 10 ft., Fly 90 ft. (hover)

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     14 |     20 |     14 |      6 |     10 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +5 |     +2 |     -2 |     +0 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +5 |     +2 |     -2 |     +0 |     -2 |

- **Resistances** Bludgeoning, Lightning, Piercing, Slashing
- **Immunities** Poison, Thunder; Exhaustion, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained, Unconscious
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 10
- **Languages** Primordial (Auran)
- **CR** 5 (XP 1,800; PB +3)

## Traits

_**Air Form.**_ The elemental can enter a creature's space and stop there. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without expending extra movement to do so.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The elemental makes two Thunderous Slam attacks.

_**Thunderous Slam.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +8, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 14 (2d8 + 5) Thunder damage.

_**Whirlwind (Recharge 4-6).**_ _Strength Saving Throw:_ DC 13, one Medium or smaller creature in the elemental's space. _Failure:_ 24 (4d10 + 2) Thunder damage, and the target is pushed up to 20 feet straight away from the elemental and has the Prone condition. _Success:_ Half damage only.
