# Hippogriff

_Large Monstrosity, Unaligned_

- **AC** 11
- **Initiative** +1 (11)
- **HP** 26 (4d10 + 4)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Fly 60 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     17 |     13 |     13 |      2 |     12 |      8 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +1 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +1 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -1 |

- **Skills** Perception +5
- **Senses** Passive Perception 15
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1 (XP 200; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Flyby.**_ The hippogriff doesn't provoke an Opportunity Attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The hippogriff makes two Rend attacks.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) Slashing damage.
