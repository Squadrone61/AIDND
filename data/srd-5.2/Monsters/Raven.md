## Traits

_**Mimicry.**_ The raven can mimic simple sounds it has heard, such as a whisper or chitter. A hearer can discern the sounds are imitations with a successful DC 10 Wisdom (Insight) check.

## Actions

_**Beak.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +4, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 1 Piercing damage.
