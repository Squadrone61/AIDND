# Young Copper Dragon

_Large Dragon (Metallic), Chaotic Good_

- **AC** 17
- **Initiative** +4 (14)
- **HP** 119 (14d10 + 42)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Climb 40 ft., Fly 80 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     12 |     17 |     16 |     13 |     15 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +1 |     +3 |     +3 |     +1 |     +2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +4 |     +3 |     +3 |     +4 |     +2 |

- **Skills** Deception +5, Perception +7, Stealth +4
- **Immunities** Acid
- **Senses** Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 120 ft.; Passive Perception 17
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **CR** 7 (XP 2,900; PB +3)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Slowing Breath.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +7, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 15 (2d10 + 4) Slashing damage.

_**Acid Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Dexterity Saving Throw:_ DC 14, each creature in a 40-foot-long, 5-footwide Line. _Failure:_ 40 (9d8) Acid damage. _Success:_ Half damage.

_**Slowing Breath.**_ _Constitution Saving Throw:_ DC 14, each creature in a 30-foot Cone. _Failure:_ The target can't take Reactions; its Speed is halved; and it can take either an action or a Bonus Action on its turn, not both. This effect lasts until the end of its next turn.
