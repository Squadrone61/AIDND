- **Initiative** +3 (13)
- **HP** 7 (2d6)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Climb 30 ft.

- **Skills** Perception +2
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 12
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1/8 (XP 25; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The rat has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the rat's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 feet. _Hit:_ 5 (1d4 + 3) Piercing damage.
