# Dretch

_Small Fiend (Demon), Chaotic Evil_

- **AC** 11
- **Initiative** +0 (10)
- **HP** 18 (4d6 + 4)
- **Speed** 20 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     12 |     11 |     12 |      5 |      8 |      3 |
| **Modifier**     |     +1 |     +0 |     +1 |     -3 |     -1 |     -4 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +1 |     +0 |     +1 |     -3 |     -1 |     -4 |

- **Resistances** Cold, Fire, Lightning
- **Immunities** Poison; Poisoned
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 9
- **Languages** Abyssal; telepathy 60 ft. (works only with creatures that understand Abyssal)
- **CR** 1/4 (XP 50; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +3, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 4 (1d6 + 1) Slashing damage.

_**Fetid Cloud (1/Day).**_ _Constitution Saving Throw:_ DC 11, each creature in a 10-foot Emanation originating from the dretch. _Failure:_ The target has the Poisoned condition until the end of its next turn. While Poisoned, the creature can take either an action or a Bonus Action on its turn, not both, and it can't take Reactions.
