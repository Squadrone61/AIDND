# Winter Wolf

_Large Monstrosity, Neutral Evil_

- **AC** 13
- **Initiative** +1 (11)
- **HP** 75 (10d10 + 20)
- **Speed** 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     18 |     13 |     14 |      7 |     12 |      8 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +1 |     +2 |     -2 |     +1 |     -1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +1 |     +2 |     -2 |     +1 |     -1 |

- **Skills** Perception +5, Stealth +5
- **Immunities** Cold
- **Senses** Passive Perception 15
- **Languages** Common, Giant
- **CR** 3 (XP 700; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The wolf has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) Piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Prone condition.

_**Cold Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Constitution Saving Throw:_ DC 12, each creature in a 15-foot Cone. _Failure:_ 18 (4d8) Cold damage. _Success:_ Half damage.
