# Giant Ape

_Huge Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 12
- **Initiative** +5 (15)
- **HP** 168 (16d12 + 64)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Climb 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     23 |     14 |     18 |      5 |     12 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +6 |     +2 |     +4 |     -3 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +6 |     +2 |     +4 |     -3 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Athletics +9, Perception +4, Survival +4
- **Senses** Passive Perception 14
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 7 (XP 2,900; PB +3)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The ape makes two Fist attacks.

_**Fist.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +9, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 22 (3d10 + 6) Bludgeoning damage.

_**Boulder Toss (Recharge 6).**_ The ape hurls a boulder at a point it can see within 90 feet. _Dexterity Saving Throw:_ DC 17, each creature in a 5-foot-radius Sphere centered on that point. _Failure:_ 24 (7d6) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Prone condition. _Success:_ Half damage only.

## Bonus Actions

_**Leap.**_ The ape jumps up to 30 feet by spending 10 feet of movement.
