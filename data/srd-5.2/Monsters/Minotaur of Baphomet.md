## Actions

_**Abyssal Glaive.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 10 (1d12 + 4) Slashing damage plus 10 (3d6) Necrotic damage.

_**Gore (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 18 (4d6 + 4) Piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature and the minotaur moved 10+ feet straight toward it immediately before the hit, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) Piercing damage and has the Prone condition.
