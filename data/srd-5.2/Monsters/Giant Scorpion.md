# Giant Scorpion

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 15
- **Initiative** +1 (11)
- **HP** 52 (7d10 + 14)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     16 |     13 |     15 |      1 |      9 |      3 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +1 |     +2 |     -5 |     -1 |     -4 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +1 |     +2 |     -5 |     -1 |     -4 |

- **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft.; Passive Perception 9
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 3 (XP 700; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The scorpion makes two Claw attacks and one Sting attack.

_**Claw.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 6 (1d6 + 3) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Grappled condition (escape DC 13) from one of two claws.

_**Sting.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) Piercing damage plus 11 (2d10) Poison damage.
