# Hobgoblin Warrior

_Medium Fey (Goblinoid), Lawful Evil_

- **AC** 18
- **Initiative** +3 (13)
- **HP** 11 (2d8 + 2)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     13 |     12 |     12 |     10 |     10 |      9 |
| **Modifier**     |     +1 |     +1 |     +1 |     +0 |     +0 |     -1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +1 |     +1 |     +1 |     +0 |     +0 |     -1 |

- **Gear** Half Plate Armor, Longbow, Longsword, Shield
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 10
- **Languages** Common, Goblin
- **CR** 1/2 (XP 100; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The hobgoblin has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hobgoblin's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

## Actions

_**Longsword.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +3, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 12 (2d10 + 1) Slashing damage.

_**Longbow.**_ _Ranged Attack Roll:_ +3, range 150/600 ft. _Hit:_ 5 (1d8 + 1) Piercing damage plus 7 (3d4) Poison damage.
