# Giant Hyena

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 12
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 45 (6d10 + 12)
- **Speed** 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     16 |     14 |     14 |      2 |     12 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +2 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +2 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 13
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1 (XP 200; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 10 (2d6 + 3) Piercing damage.

## Bonus Actions

_**Rampage (1/Day).**_ Immediately after dealing damage to a creature that was already Bloodied, the hyena can move up to half its Speed, and it makes one Bite attack.
