# Spider

_Tiny Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 12
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 1 (1d4 - 1)
- **Speed** 20 ft., Climb 20 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      2 |     14 |      8 |      1 |     10 |      2 |
| **Modifier**     |     -4 |     +2 |     -1 |     -5 |     +0 |     -4 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -4 |     +2 |     -1 |     -5 |     +0 |     -4 |

- **Skills** Stealth +4
- **Senses** Darkvision 30 ft.; Passive Perception 10
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 0 (XP 10; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Spider Climb.**_ The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

_**Web Walker.**_ The spider ignores movement restrictions caused by webs, and the spider knows the location of any other creature in contact with the same web.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +4, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 1 Piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) Poison damage.
