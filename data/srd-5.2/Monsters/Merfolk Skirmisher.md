## Traits

_**Amphibious.**_ The merfolk can breathe air and water.

## Actions

_**Ocean Spear.**_ _Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:_ +2, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft. _Hit:_ 3 (1d6) Piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) Cold damage. If the target is a creature, its Speed decreases by 10 feet until the end of its next turn. _Hit or Miss:_ The spear magically returns to the merfolk's hand immediately after a ranged attack.
