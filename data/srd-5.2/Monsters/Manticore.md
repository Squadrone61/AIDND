# Manticore

_Large Monstrosity, Lawful Evil_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** +3 (13)
- **HP** 68 (8d10 + 24)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Fly 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     17 |     16 |     17 |      7 |     12 |      8 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +3 |     +3 |     -2 |     +1 |     -1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +3 |     +3 |     -2 |     +1 |     -1 |

- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 11
- **Languages** Common
- **CR** 3 (XP 700; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The manticore makes three attacks, using Rend or Tail Spike in any combination.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) Slashing damage.

_**Tail Spike.**_ _Ranged Attack Roll:_ +5, range 100/200 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) Piercing damage.
