# Giant Boar

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 13
- **Initiative** +0 (10)
- **HP** 42 (5d10 + 15)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|

| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +0 |     +3 |     -4 |     -2 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +5 |     +0 |     +3 |     -4 |     -2 |     -3 |

- **Senses** Passive Perception 8
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 2 (XP 450; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Bloodied Fury.**_ The boar has Advantage on melee attack rolls while it is Bloodied.

## Actions

_**Gore.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +5, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 10 (2d6 + 3) Piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature and the boar moved 20+ feet straight toward it immediately before the hit, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) Piercing damage and has the Prone condition.
