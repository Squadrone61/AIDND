- **Initiative** +1 (11)
- **HP** 22 (3d10 + 6)
- **Speed** 60 ft.

| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +1 |     +2 |     -4 |     -1 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +1 |     +2 |     -4 |     -1 |     -3 |

- **Immunities** Poison; Exhaustion, Poisoned
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 9
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1/2 (XP 100; PB +2)

## Actions

_**Hooves.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d6 + 4) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature and the skeleton moved 20+ feet straight toward it immediately before the hit, the target has the Prone condition.
