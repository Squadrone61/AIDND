- **Languages** None
- **CR** 0 (XP 0; PB +2)

## Reactions

_**Shriek.**_ _Trigger:_ A creature or a source of Bright Light moves within 30 feet of the shrieker. _Response:_ The shrieker emits a shriek audible within 300 feet of itself for 1 minute or until the shrieker dies.
