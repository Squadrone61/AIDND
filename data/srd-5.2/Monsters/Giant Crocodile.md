# Giant Crocodile

_Huge Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** -1 (9)
- **HP** 85 (9d12 + 27)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Swim 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     21 |      9 |     17 |      2 |     10 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +5 |     -1 |     +3 |     -4 |     +0 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +5 |     -1 |     +3 |     -4 |     +0 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Stealth +5
- **Senses** Passive Perception 10
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 5 (XP 1,800; PB +3)

## Traits

_**Hold Breath.**_ The crocodile can hold its breath for 1 hour.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The crocodile makes one Bite attack and one Tail attack.

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +8, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 21 (3d10 + 5) Piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Grappled condition (escape DC 15). While Grappled, the target has the Restrained condition and can't be targeted by the crocodile's Tail.

_**Tail.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +8, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 5) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Prone condition.
