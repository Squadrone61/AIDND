# Young Black Dragon

_Large Dragon (Chromatic), Chaotic Evil_

- **AC** 18
- **Initiative** +5 (15)
- **HP** 127 (15d10 + 45)
- **Speed** 40 ft., Fly 80 ft., Swim 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     14 |     17 |     12 |     11 |     15 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +2 |     +3 |     +1 |     +0 |     +2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +5 |     +3 |     +1 |     +3 |     +2 |

- **Skills** Perception +6, Stealth +5
- **Immunities** Acid
- **Senses** Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 120 ft.; Passive Perception 16
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **CR** 7 (XP 2,900; PB +3)

## Traits

_**Amphibious.**_ The dragon can breathe air and water.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The dragon makes three Rend attacks.

_**Rend.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +7, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 9 (2d4 + 4) Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Acid damage.

_**Acid Breath (Recharge 5-6).**_ _Dexterity Saving Throw:_ DC 14, each creature in a 30-foot-long, 5-footwide Line. _Failure:_ 49 (14d6) Acid damage. _Success:_ Half damage.
