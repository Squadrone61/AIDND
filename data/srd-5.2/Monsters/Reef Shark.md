# Reef Shark

_Medium Beast, Unaligned_

- **AC** 12
- **Initiative** +2 (12)
- **HP** 22 (4d8 + 4)
- **Speed** 5 ft., Swim 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     14 |     15 |     13 |      1 |     10 |      4 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +2 |     +1 |     -5 |     +0 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +2 |     +1 |     -5 |     +0 |     -3 |

- **Skills** Perception +2
- **Senses** Blindsight 30 ft.; Passive Perception 12
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 1/2 (XP 100; PB +2)

## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The shark has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the shark's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

_**Water Breathing.**_ The shark can breathe only underwater.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +4, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (2d4 + 2) Piercing damage.
