## Traits

_**Bloodied Fury.**_ While Bloodied, the boar has Advantage on attack rolls.

## Actions

_**Gore.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +3, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 4 (1d6 + 1) Piercing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature and the boar moved 20+ feet straight toward it immediately before the hit, the target takes an extra 3 (1d6) Piercing damage and has the Prone condition.
