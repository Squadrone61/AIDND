# Wyvern

_Large Dragon, Unaligned_

- **AC** 14
- **Initiative** +0 (10)
- **HP** 127 (15d10 + 45)
- **Speed** 30 ft., Fly 80 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     10 |     16 |      5 |     12 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +0 |     +3 |     -3 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +0 |     +3 |     -3 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +4
- **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft.; Passive Perception 14
- **Languages** None
- **CR** 6 (XP 2,300; PB +3)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The wyvern makes one Bite attack and one Sting attack.

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +7, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 13 (2d8 + 4) Piercing damage.

_**Sting.**_ _Melee Attack Roll:_ +7, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) Piercing damage plus 24 (7d6) Poison damage, and the target has the Poisoned condition until the start of the wyvern's next turn.
