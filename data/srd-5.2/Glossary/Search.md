# Search \[Action\]

When you take the Search action, you make a Wisdom check to discern something that isn't obvious. The table suggests which skills are applicable when you take this action, depending on what you're trying to detect.

| Skill      | Thing to Detect                      |
|------------|--------------------------------------|
| Insight    | Creature's state of mind             |
| Medicine   | Creature's ailment or cause of death |
| Perception | Concealed creature or object         |
| Survival   | Tracks or food                       |
