# Paralyzed \[Condition\]

While you have the Paralyzed condition, you experience the following effects.

_**Incapacitated.**_ You have the Incapacitated condition.

_**Speed 0.**_ Your Speed is 0 and can't increase.

_**Saving Throws Affected.**_ You automatically fail Strength and Dexterity saving throws.

_**Attacks Affected.**_ Attack rolls against you have Advantage.

_**Automatic Critical Hits.**_ Any attack roll that hits you is a Critical Hit if the attacker is within 5 feet of you.
