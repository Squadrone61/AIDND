# Spare the Dying

_Necromancy Cantrip (Cleric, Druid)_

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 15 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature within range that has 0 Hit Points and isn't dead. The creature becomes Stable.

_**Cantrip Upgrade.**_ The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).
