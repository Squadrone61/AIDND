# Longstrider

_Level 1 Transmutation (Bard, Druid, Ranger, Wizard)_

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dirt)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a creature. The target's Speed increases by 10 feet until the spell ends.

_**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.**_ You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.
